How to Use Custom Browser Tabs

Welcome to the Custom Browser Tabs page! This page allows you to manage multiple browser tabs within a single window.

Getting Started

  1. Click on the Tab 1 to see this introduction.
  2. Click on the + button to create a new tab.
  3. In the new tab, enter the URL of the website you want to visit and press Enter.
  4. You can switch between tabs by clicking on the tab names.
  5. To close a tab, click on the x button.
